Track your Robinhood portfolio from your desktop.

Open source, free, fully native and secure.

Download Robinhood for Mac

Installation

Mac native version of Robinhood.
Simply download the Robinhood for mac .dmg file and install it.
After installation create a StockStalk account and log in with your Robinhood credentials.
Your Mac title bar will show an icon with your current daily portfolio performance, clicking on it will open the Robinhood desktop app.
